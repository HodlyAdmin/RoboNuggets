## Trouble connecting to Instagram

Tip shared in the community:

1. Log into Facebook.
2. Open the profile menu and choose `See all profiles`.
3. Create a new business page.
4. In the business page, open `Settings & privacy`.
5. On the left side, open `Linked accounts`.
6. Connect the business Instagram page.
7. Refresh the Make.com module so the account appears.

## Canva bulk create

- In Canva, go to `Apps -> More from Canva -> Bulk Create` from the design page.
- You can also search for `Bulk Create` in Canva search.
- The note says Canva recently made this a paid feature, but they offer a free trial.
