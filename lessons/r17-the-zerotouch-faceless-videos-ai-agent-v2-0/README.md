Video Ideas Prompt

Tweak the prompt below for your intended niche:

You provide detailed video prompts for AI generation tools like Midjourney and Flux

You have extensive experience in this area




Your task: 

Output a table and just the table with the following columns and values




id - going from 1 and incrementing by 1 per row

production_status - should all be "todo"

publish_status - should all be "todo"

title - short title phrase that is less than 50 characters. no emojis

caption - short caption that is less than 120 characters, no emojis, hashtags are ok. Make it friendly to the viewer and do not sound robotic

prompt_a - first video prompt that aligns with the title

prompt_b  - second video prompt that aligns with the title, this should be a different character than prompt a

prompt_c - third video prompt that aligns with the title, this should be a different character than prompt a or b

action_a - a short action for the character in prompt_a

action_b - a short action for the character in prompt_b, should be different versus action_a

action_c - a short action for the character in prompt_c, should be different versus action_b and action_c




NEVER use double-quotes for any of the values







-----




Here are the specs for the video prompts




Quantity of rows I need: 10

Minimum prompt length: 100 words

Maximum action prompt length: 15 words

My video niche: female Disney characters in 80s panavision style




ALWAYS include these keywords in the prompts:

-80s style

-real-life version

-panavision

-25 year old

-1girl




Other guidance for the prompts (feel free to paraphrase this area):

-Emotion: Ethereal, enchanting, real-life, curious eyes, mischievous, wonder.

-Appearance: intricate patterns

-Setting: cinematic, soft bokeh, glowing, nostalgia.

-Pose: waist-up, warm lighting, cinematic, richly detailed, soft shadows, subtle film grain, glowing highlights, attractive, perfect body

-Inspiration: 80s fantasy films, The NeverEnding Story, Legend

-Technical Details: Panavision lens, ultra-realistic, high-definition, awe-inspiring  --v 5, --q 2, --style 80s cinematic.



Example of a good video prompt:
An ethereal and enchanting version of a real-life version of a 24-year old Tinkerbell in an 80s Panavision cinematic style, glowing softly in a warm magical forest setting. She has delicate, translucent fairy wings with intricate patterns that shimmer in the light, wearing a shimmering emerald-green dress that reflects the ambient glow. Framed in a classic 'cowboy shot' from the waist up, her youthful face is framed by golden-blonde hair styled with soft, feathery bangs, and she has expressive, curious eyes that sparkle with mischief and wonder. The lighting is warm and cinematic, with soft bokeh effects in the background, capturing the essence of classic 80s fantasy films like 'The NeverEnding Story' and 'Legend'. Shot with a dreamy Panavision lens, the image is richly detailed, with soft shadows, subtle film grain, and glowing highlights that evoke nostalgia. Ultra-realistic, high-definition, and awe-inspiring. --ar 16:9 --v 5 --q 2 --style 80s cinematic
